SCCM Child Care

In addition to our Integrated Early Childhood and Preschool classrooms, we offer our exclusive Toddler rooms where we provide the same curriculum, staff and belief system and experiences to 18 month- 3 year-olds. For families in need of more child care, we offer before and after school care. Although our formal day is scheduled from 8:30-1:30, we continue to provide child-directed learning activities and experiences in the morning as well as in the afternoon. We very much value this additional time with your child and are thrilled to have the opportunity to offer extended learning opportunities and expansion projects driven from our enhanced curriculum.

"Child care, also known as early childhood education and care", is mainly designed for children aged up to five years, but can also include before-school, after-school and holiday care for primary school-age children. This is often called outside school hours care.

The Australian Government is investing additional funding into the new Child Care Package to provide more support for families. Implemented on 2 July 2018, it represents the most significant change to the early childhood and care system in 40 years. This has provide us an extra support to uplift this system in new and advance way to communicate and understand children so that we can provide them our best to make their childhood most enjoyable and knowledgeble.
