About This Organization

Founded By

Somerset Community Care Matters started in 2010 and in 2011 was launched as a project at MindTWS.
The Launch was attended by the Mayor, Steve Brooks
In 2015 SCCM became independent of Mind
Our Founder, Brenda Prentice, received a National Award for Unpaid Carers at the Great British Carers Award in 2013 at The Park Lane Hotel in London. The category was sponsored by Nuticia.
It is governed by a Board of Trustees in accordance with Charity Commission Guidance.

Welcome to SCCM Childcare Facility

"Where We Reach for Stars, Hand in Hand, One Step at a Time”

It is a great pleasure to welcome you to SCCM Childcare Facility Website. SCCM Child Care name was picked because it was meant to be the best, and that’s what we strive to be. Your child will receive the best love, care, guidance and education that we would give as to our own. We are a 4 star rating, Title III Honor Roll learning center located in Paradis, Louisiana – Providing 20 years of quality and affordable childcare, preschool education, before and after school care and our annual theme summer camp.

Our full-time childcare center offers high quality care for children ages 6 weeks to school-aged. Our pre-school program is designed for ages 1- 4 to prepare them for their all-important school years ahead. Children participate in a variety of individual and group activities designed to develop language and reading readiness skills enhancing physical, social, emotional and intellectual growth, skills and attitudes which will increase their ability to succeed in school.
