About This Organization

Founded By

Somerset Community Care Matters started in 2010 and in 2011 was launched as a project at MindTWS.
The Launch was attended by the Mayor, Steve Brooks
In 2015 SCCM became independent of Mind
Our Founder, Brenda Prentice, received a National Award for Unpaid Carers at the Great British Carers Award in 2013 at The Park Lane Hotel in London. The category was sponsored by Nuticia.
It is governed by a Board of Trustees in accordance with Charity Commission Guidance.
